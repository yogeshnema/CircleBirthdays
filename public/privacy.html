Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to Purawale. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Purawale is a private family platform. We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, phone number (used for authentication), date of birth, and family relationships.
  • Media and Content: Photos, stories, heritage recipes, and other media you upload to the Gallery or Memory Lane.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our private messaging system.
  • Location Data: Geographical information for the Global Family Map, provided voluntarily to show where family members are located.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Purawale platform.
  • Facilitate family connections and the Member Directory.
  • Enable private messaging between family members.
  • Display the Global Family Map.
  • Preserve family heritage through recipes and stories.
  • Authenticate your account via phone-based login.

3. Data Infrastructure and Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase to power our application infrastructure:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages secure phone-based login.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores relational data, social content, and directory information.
  • Cloud Storage: Stores images and media uploads.

4. Data Sharing and Access Control

Purawale is a "family-only" private network. Your data is only accessible to authenticated members of the Purawale family group. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting the family administrator or using the in-app support features.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process, leveraging the security features of the Firebase platform.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact us through the app's internal messaging or via the family administrator.
